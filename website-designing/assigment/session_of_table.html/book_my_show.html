Steps to Book a Movie Ticket on BookMyShow

  1. Open the BookMyShow app or website and select your city.
  2. Search for the movie you want to watch.
  3. Select the preferred date, showtime, and cinema theatre.
  4. Choose your desired seats from the seat layout map.
  5. Review your booking details and proceed to checkout.
  6. Select a payment method (UPI, Debit/Credit Card, Net Banking) and complete the payment.
